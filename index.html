About Me

I am a Senior Software Engineer, certified AWS Solutions Architect, Web developer and Consultant. In addition to that, I am an award winning songwriter with a background in audio engineering. After spending years travelling the world as a recording artist and touring act, I began to think outside of my own box and explore my personal interest in technology and the way it shapes our lives. My aha moment came in the form of conversations with the mentors in my life who were working in the field, encouraging me to explore the possibilities. I took a leap into software engineering, and haven't looked back since. I graduated from Fullstack Academy and have spent the last 3 years honing my skills as a lead developer, contributing to a team working to secure client file sharing capabilities, creating intuitive features for web applications and designing scalable systems that advance innovation.
